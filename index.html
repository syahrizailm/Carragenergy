What is Carragenergy?

Carragenergy is a eco-friendly yet superior battery compared with Li-Ion AA battery. Carragenergy came as an idea as an energy shift towards greener and more sustainable Electrochemical Energy Storage.

Our Technology

Carragenergy is an organic rechargeable battery with organic electrode, recyclable components, and biopolymer electrolyte from red seaweed extract named Carragenean. Watch our video to see how our prototype work.
